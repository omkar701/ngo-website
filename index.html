Our Journey So Far

From humble beginnings to a widespread impact, Navi Disha Foundation has steadily grown, touching thousands of lives across various communities. Here are some key milestones:

2010 - Foundation Established

Navi Disha Foundation officially registered, beginning with a focus on primary education in rural areas.

2014 - Healthcare Initiatives Launched

Expanded our reach to include medical camps and health awareness programs in underserved villages.

2018 - Livelihood Programs Begin

Introduced vocational training and microfinance support to empower women and youth economically.

2022 - Environmental Projects Initiated

Launched large-scale tree plantation drives and waste management campaigns.
